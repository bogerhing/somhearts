Our Story

Born in Los Angeles

A Brand Born From
A Quiet Belief

Soma Hearts was founded in Los Angeles in 2019 by a woman who believed that what you wear to bed matters just as much as what you wear to work. Not because of appearances — but because of how it makes you feel.

We started with a simple question: why is it so hard to find nightwear that feels truly luxurious without costing a fortune? Nightwear that isn't just functional, but genuinely beautiful. Nightwear you'd be happy to be seen in.

The answer became Soma Hearts — a collection of premium sleepwear designed for the woman who believes the quietest moments of her day deserve to feel special.
