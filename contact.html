Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unworn with tags attached. Please contact us to initiate a return.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $75 within the contiguous United States.

How do I care for my Soma pieces?

Most pieces are machine washable on a gentle, cold-water cycle. Satin pieces should be hand washed. Care instructions are on each product tag.

What sizes do you carry?

We carry XS through 3XL across our collection. Each product page includes a detailed size guide to help you find your perfect fit.
